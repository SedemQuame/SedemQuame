What needed to exist

Friends wanted a light-weight, invite-only experience focused on safety and context. That meant building onboarding that collected just enough metadata for smart matching, plus tooling to weed out duplicate or malicious profiles without manual moderation.

Android experience

  • Crafted onboarding wizard with progressive disclosure and photo verification.
  • Built match feed, in-app chat, and push notifications using Firebase Cloud Messaging.
  • Leveraged Material motion + dark mode support for accessibility.

Backend + AI

  • Implemented Node.js + MongoDB APIs for auth, profiles, and recommendations.
  • Used OCR to detect unsafe content and convert images to searchable tags.
  • Automated deployment to Heroku with CI smoke tests.
