Maaze Game

A Unity-based action puzzle shooter with procedural levels, dynamic AI, and over 1,000 downloads across PC and Android marketplaces.

Role

Game Developer

Timeline

Jan 2021 – Nov 2021

Stack

Unity, C#, Blender, Cinemachine

Impact

1K+ downloads · Positive player retention · Playtested with closed community cohorts

Google Play itch.io build

Creative direction

Maaze blends puzzle solving with fast-paced shooting. I handled everything from systems design to art direction, ensuring each level felt handcrafted while still being generated procedurally.

Gameplay systems

  • Implemented AI-driven enemies and NPCs using behavior trees and state machines.
  • Added real-time difficulty adjustments so sessions stayed challenging yet fair.
  • Used Cinemachine for polished camera work, including screen shakes and focus pulls.

Performance

  • Optimized assets with LOD systems, object pooling, and asynchronous loading.
  • Maintained parity across PC and Android builds with targeted graphics profiles.
  • Shipped telemetry hooks to track retention and inform balancing patches.

Distribution

Released Maaze on itch.io and Google Play, then iterated through Discord-based tester groups, classroom demos, and structured survey loops to gather actionable feedback before the public launch.

Key contributions

Art pipeline

Modeled and textured assets inside Blender, ensuring cohesive lighting and tone.

Tooling

Built internal editors for quest logic and spawn rules to accelerate iteration.

Community

Created dev logs and tutorials that later fed into Medium posts and workshops.
