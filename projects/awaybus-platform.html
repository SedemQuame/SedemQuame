Problem

Travelers needed a clear view of routes, seat availability, and payment confirmation, while operations teams demanded accurate manifests synced with depot systems. Manual processes were leading to overbooking and support load.

Experience

  • Designed responsive flows covering discovery, booking, payment, and ticket retrieval.
  • Built a seat-selection module that mirrored the physical layout of each bus class.
  • Added localized copy and currency support for regional markets.

Platform

  • Implemented REST APIs that connected booking, payments, and dispatch subsystems.
  • Created admin views so operators could reschedule buses, trigger refunds, and monitor capacity.
  • Instrumented logging + alerts to flag payment discrepancies before they hit customers.
